FAQs
What is ASTRA Scholarship?

ANALOG IAS Academy conducts a test every year for Civil Services aspirants who are about to start their preparation. Through this exam, aspirants can avail discount in the tuition fee for Civils Services coaching provided by ANALOG IAS.

How to Register for the Scholarship Test?

Register online.

What is the Registration Fee?

Fee to get the eligibility to write Scholarship Test is ₹250/-

Benefits upon paying Registration Fee?

  • Eligible to write scholarship test
  • Free NCERT short video courses.
  • Mentorship to help in preparation for scholarship test and also in starting Civils preparation before joining coaching classes.
  • Information Booklet with details about the pattern and syllabus of the Civils examination.

Why should I write Scholarship Test?

  • Earn fee discount on Civils Coaching (both offline & online classes)
  • Gain a fair idea about the pattern of examination.
  • Self-assess your knowledge.

Scholarship Test Syllabus?

Indian Polity, Ancient History, Medieval History, Modern History, Economy, Geography, General Knowledge, Arithmetic and Reasoning, English Comprehension, Science and Technology and Environment.

Test Duration and mode?

Duration is 120 min.
Test Mode - Online

Marking Scheme and Penalty?

Each question carries 1 Mark. No negative marking.

When will results be declared?

Results will be declared within 48 hrs from the day of the test.

Online Test Portal and Login Details?

You will receive Portal Link and login Id and Password via SMS 48 hrs before the Examination Day.

Where can I Take the Test?

As the test is in Online mode,you can take the test from place of your choice with uninterrupted Internet connection.

System Requirements for Online Test?

Devise can be of your choice – Smart Phone (Android/iOS) or Tab or Desktop or Laptop. However, desktops and laptops are preferred as they offer convenience to read and attempt a question without the interruption of phone calls.

What is the Medium of the Test?

Test will be held only in English Medium.

What if I forgot or deleted SMS of Login Credentials?

Don’t worry, we will be sending the SMS multiple times until the test commencement. And anyway you have an option of calling us directly for any information.

How I can directly contact ANALOG IAS Administration?
