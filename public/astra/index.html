FAQs
What is ASTRA Scholarship?

ANALOG IAS Academy, conducts every year a Test to Civil Services Aspirants who is just about to start their preparation. Though this exam aspirants can avail discount in the Tuition fee for Civils Services Coaching provided by ANALOG IAS.

How to Register for the Scholarship Test?

Can Register Online using the link #{URI}

What is the Registration Fee?

Fee to get the eligibility to write Scholarship Test is 250/-

Benefits upon paying Registration Fee?

  • Eligible to write Scholarship Test
  • Current Affairs Materials PDFs
  • NCERT Short Video Course
  • Counselling opportunity to get to know completely about Civils Services Examination.
  • Mentorship to help in preparation for Scholarship Test and also in starting Civils Preparation before joining Coaching Classes.
  • Information Booklet which details about the pattern and Syllabus of the Civils examination.

Why should I write Scholarship Test?

  • Can get Fee Discount on Civils Coaching. Discount can avail on both Offline and Online Course Fee as per your choice.
  • You will get a fair idea about pattern of Civils Examination.
  • Self-access amount of knowledge you have on Civil Services Examination Subjects.

Scholarship Test Syllabus?

Subjects as in Civil Services Syllabus - Indian Polity, Ancient History, Medieval History, Modern History, Economy, Geography, General Knowledge, Arithmetic and Reasoning, English Comprehension, Science and Technology and Environment.

Test Duration and mode?

Duration is 2Hrs or 120 min.
Test Mode - Online

Marking Scheme and Penalty?

Each question carries 1 Mark.
Wrong answer/Unattempt Question – NO negative Marking.

When will results be declared?

Results will be declared within 48Hrs from the day of the Scholarship test.

Online Test Portal and Login Details?

You will receive Portal Link and login Id and Password via SMS 48Hrs before the Examination Day.

In which place I can write Online Test?

As the test is in Online mode, can take the test from place of your choice with uninterrupted Internet connection.

System Requirements to write Online Test?

Devise can be of your choice which have Internet connection or access – Smart Phone (Android/iOS) or Tab or Desktop or Laptop. However, desktops and laptops are preferred as they offer convenience to read and attempt a question also no interruptions like phone calls in Smart Phone.

What is the Language medium of the Test?

Test will be held only in English Medium.

What if I forgot or deleted SMS of Login Credentials?

Don’t worry we will be sending the SMS multiple times including just before the Test scheduled starting time. And anyway you have an option of calling us directly to get any information including Login Credentials.
